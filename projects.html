Projects That Matter

At Higher Theory Dev, we build more than code — we create real products designed to become thriving businesses. Each project here is a journey you can follow, from idea to launch.

Click on any project to dive into the full series of tutorials and see how these ideas come to life.

Klipshow

A tool for Twitch streamers that lets viewers pay to trigger approved short clips from social platforms. Streamers control the queue — Klipshow never sells or hosts the content itself.

Smash Factory

Brings real-time interaction to Smash Bros Twitch streams. Viewers can support the channel by donating tokens to trigger item drops mid-game.

Live Arcade

A gamified support system for Twitch streamers. Viewers purchase tokens to play arcade games on stream — when they set a new high score, their name is broadcast live. Every token supports the channel.

Byte Sized

Short-form videos that cut to the chase — simple explanations, powerful takeaways, and dev knowledge that sticks.
