Live Arcade

A gamified support system for Twitch streamers. Viewers purchase tokens to play arcade games on stream — when they set a new high score, their name is broadcast live. Every token supports the channel.

How It Works

Live Arcade brings your viewers front and center by turning support into gameplay. They buy tokens to play arcade-style games directly on stream — and when they hit a high score, their name takes the spotlight live.

Designed for Streamers

Everything is opt-in and customizable. You choose which games are active, set limits on play, and control how shout-outs appear. It’s all about boosting engagement without losing control of your stream.

Under the Hood

Built using Electron for the desktop overlay, with a backend powered by Node.js and WebSockets for real-time updates. Game logic is written in TypeScript, and all player data is stored securely using Supabase.
